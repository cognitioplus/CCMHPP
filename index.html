PFA Model: Look, Listen, Link

Module 3

Hover over each stage to understand your specific responsibilities during a crisis interaction. PFA is a non-intrusive approach to support.

1

LOOK

Check for safety & urgent needs.

  • Check for safety (environment & person).
  • Check for obvious urgent basic needs.
  • Check for serious distress reactions.
2

LISTEN

Offer support & calm.

  • Approach people who may need support.
  • Ask about needs and concerns.
  • Listen and help them feel calm.
3

LINK

Connect to services.

  • Help address basic needs/services.
  • Give information.
  • Connect with loved ones and social support.
  • Warm Handoff: Call resource with client present.
