9.2 Renewal Requirements

The certification is valid for two years. Maintaining your credential requires ongoing commitment to professional standards.

Renewal Requirements

Requirement Details Frequency
Refresher Course Minimum 6-hour DOH-recognized refresher course Every 2 years
Case Reflections Submission of 3 new, supervised case reflections Every 2 years
Mentor Endorsement Confirmation of continued ethical practice Every 2 years
Continuing Education 12 hours of approved CPD activities Every 2 years

Approved CPD Activities

  • Workshops and seminars on mental health
  • Participation in supervision groups
  • Conference attendance
  • Advanced training courses
  • Case consultation sessions
  • Mental health advocacy activities
